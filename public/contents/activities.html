Our Activities

At NFCS AE-FUNAI, we live the faith by worshipping God in routine activities together with the community.

Sunday Mass

"Remember to keep the sabbath day holy." (Ex. 20:8). We have mass every sunday at the church building site by 7.00 AM.

Weekday Mass

"... Do this in memory of me" (Lk. 22:19). We also have weekday masses every Tuesday and Friday morning by 6.30 AM.

Confession

Confessions hold during the weekdays. Confessions take place immediately after weekday masses.

Adoration

We adore our Lord Jesus Christ in the Blessed Sacrament bimonthly on the first and last Fridays of each month by 5.00 PM.

Morning Prayers

We gather together every Saturday morning by 6.30 AM to pray and thank God for his blessings during the week.

Prayer Meetings

Members of pious societies gather at various times to carry out holy and heaven-reaching prayers and devotions.

We have great records, very great ones, greater than you could imagine. It has been all God, because what God cannot do does not exist.
